Usage Instructions

Mouse Controls: Click and drag to pan, mouse wheel to zoom, Shift+drag to move individual images, Ctrl/Cmd+hover for 8x magnifier

Keyboard Shortcuts: v (simple wipe), w (full wipe), a/b (show single image), u/o/d/i (composite modes), c (checkerboard), r (reset), h (help)

File Loading: Click A/B boxes to upload, or drag and drop images anywhere in the application
